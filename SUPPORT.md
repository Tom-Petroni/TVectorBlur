# Support

Commercial support is provided to licensed customers.

## Channels
- support@tsuite.tools

## What to include in tickets
- TSuite version
- Node name
- Nuke version
- OS + GPU details
- steps to reproduce
- sample script or media when possible

## Severity Guidelines
- P1: production blocking, no workaround
- P2: major degradation, limited workaround
- P3: minor issue or documentation gap

## Typical Response Targets
- P1: same business day
- P2: 1 business day
- P3: 3 business days

Exact SLA depends on your contract tier.
